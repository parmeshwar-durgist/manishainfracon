20 Common Inquiries Answered

We manufacture high-grade basalt stone aggregates in sizes: 80 MM, 60 MM, 40 MM, 20 MM, 10 MM, and 6 MM. We also produce Granular Sub Base (GSB), Crush Sand (M-Sand), Cone Sand (masonry plaster sand), and washed sand.

VSI (Vertical Shaft Impact) aggregates are shaped by high-velocity rock-on-rock collisions, creating cubical particles. This shape improves concrete workability, reduces cement/sand demands, and increases compressive strength.

GSB is a blend of crushed stone aggregates and rock dust. It serves as the primary load-bearing foundation layer in road and highway construction, distributing the wheel load and facilitating water drainage.

Crush Sand (M-Sand) is manufactured by crushing hard blue basalt rocks and is graded to meet zone-II sand standards. It contains no organic silt or clay impurities, providing stronger bonding in concrete structures compared to river sand.

Cone Sand is fine sand processed through cone crushers. It is designed to meet plastering and masonry mortar specifications, ensuring a smooth wall finish and reducing plaster cracks.

Wash Sand is M-Sand that has been washed twice in our washing plant to extract basalt dust particles. This ensures cleaner concrete mixing and maximizes structural compression limits.

All our basalt aggregates and manufactured sands comply with Indian Standard codes (IS:383) and Ministry of Road Transport & Highways (MORTH) road specifications.

To ensure logistical efficiency, our standard minimum order quantity for basalt aggregates, GSB, and manufactured sand is 10 metric tons.
