Who We Are

Class One Government Contractor for RMC & Crusher Materials

Established in 2010, Manisha Infracon Project Pvt. Ltd. is a premier Class One Government Approved Contractor and major infrastructure materials supplier based in Ahilyanagar. We operate fully automated Ready Mix Concrete (RMC) batching plants and high-speed basalt stone crushing units. Sourcing raw materials from high-density blue basalt quarries, we guarantee certified structural concrete, MORTH-compliant aggregates, and double-washed manufactured sand (M-Sand).

Our Operations

The Engineering of Concrete & Aggregates

We maintain strict quality control at every phase of production. Our blue basalt stone quarries feed automated crushing lines to make perfectly cubical aggregates, while our automated RMC batching facilities use precision weighing and twin-shaft mixers to mix concrete matching exact target design grades.

Computerized SCADA twin-shaft mixing ensures absolute uniformity, correct water-cement slump ratios, and certified compressive strengths.
