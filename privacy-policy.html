At Manisha Infracon Project Pvt. Ltd., we prioritize the privacy and security of our clients, partners, and site visitors. This Privacy Policy details how we collect, handle, and safeguard your data when utilizing our services and website.

1. Information Collection

We collect personal information that you provide to us directly through contact forms, request-for-quote modals, and email communication. This information includes:

  • Full Name and Company Details.
  • Phone Numbers and Email Addresses.
  • Delivery Site Locations and Tonnage Demands.
  • Any specific queries or message details submitted to our representatives.

Additionally, we collect standard log data through cookies and tracking systems to analyze website traffic, session durations, and page performance.

2. Usage of Collected Information

The information gathered is utilized exclusively to manage corporate requests and ensure logistics delivery:

  • Processing quote requests for Ready Mix Concrete (RMC) grades and crusher stone aggregates.
  • Coordinating transit mixer trucks and tipper fleets to your specific coordinates.
  • Addressing customer support, contract enquiries, and newsletter updates.
  • Improving our system mechanics, site usability, and quality assurances.

3. Data Security and Storage

We employ robust administrative, technical, and physical safety protocols to prevent unauthorized access, manipulation, or leakage of your private records. Your data is stored on secure servers with restricted personnel authorization limits. While we utilize state-of-the-art encryption methods, please note that no internet-based data transmission holds absolute security guarantees.

4. Disclosure to Third Parties

Manisha Infracon Project Pvt. Ltd. does not trade, sell, or rent your private identification data to third-party marketing entities. We may share details with trusted logistics partners, sub-contractors, or banking processors solely as required to execute deliveries or process contractual agreements.

5. Policy Updates

We reserve the right to modify or replace this Privacy Policy at any time without prior notifications. Changes are immediately active upon publishing to this URL. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about our data protection standards.
