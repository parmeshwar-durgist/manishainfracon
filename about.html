Company Overview

Class One Government Contractor for RMC & Crusher Materials

Established in 2010, Manisha Infracon Project Pvt. Ltd. stands as a premier Class One Government Approved Contractor and concrete supplier in the Ahilyanagar region. Over the years, we have evolved from a leading basalt stone crushing unit to a fully integrated infrastructure partner, combining a heavy aggregate quarry division with a state-of-the-art Ready Mix Concrete (RMC) batching division.

We operate automated heavy crusher plants utilizing primary jaw crushers, secondary cone crushers, and high-speed Vertical Shaft Impact (VSI) shaping machinery, producing premium aggregates and washed sand (M-Sand). Simultaneously, our fully automated RMC batching plant produces high-performance concrete ranging from standard structural mixes to specialized self-compacting, green, and fiber-reinforced concrete. This dual capacity makes us the trusted Class One supplier for massive national highways (MORTH specifications), bridges, high-rises, and Individual House Builder (IHB) projects.

Our Journey

Key Milestones & Growth History

2010 - Establishment

Crusher Plant Commissioned

We inaugurated our primary blue basalt quarry mining and crushing units in Ahilyanagar, starting our journey as a high-grade aggregate manufacturer.

2015 - VSI Upgrade

VSI & M-Sand Integration

We introduced high-speed VSI shaping machinery and double sand-washing plants to manufacture premium cubical aggregates and eco-friendly manufactured plaster sand.

2020 - RMC Scaling

Ready Mix Concrete & Class One Expansion

Added our computerized Ready Mix Concrete (RMC) batching units, achieving Class One Government Approved Concrete Contractor status with over 50 transit mixers.

Quality Commitment

At Manisha Infracon, quality is a non-negotiable metric. Our on-site testing facility is equipped with automated testing rigs to evaluate aggregate impact values (AIV), aggregate crushing values (ACV), flakiness indexes, and sieve gradings.

We ensure every metric ton of basalt aggregate or manufactured sand dispatched complies with **IS:383** structural standards and Ministry of Road Transport & Highways (MORTH) road specifications.

Safety Standards

We adhere strictly to DGMS (Directorate General of Mines Safety) guidelines to ensure quarry mining safety. Every employee receives regular safety drill training, personal protective equipment (PPE) suites, and heavy machinery training.

Our site operations incorporate advanced dust suppression systems and regular machinery checks to maintain a healthy work environment.
